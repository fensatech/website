Features
Everything your family needs

Shared Family Calendar

One calendar, every member. See everyone's events at a glance — colour-coded by family member so nothing gets lost in the noise.

School Appointments

Track parent-teacher meetings, school events, pick-up times, and term dates — all in one place, with automatic reminders before they sneak up on you.

Medical & Health

Store vaccination records, track upcoming check-ups, log prescriptions, and manage medical appointments for every family member in one secure place.

Family Member Profiles

Create profiles for each family member — kids, adults, even grandparents. Assign events, track individual needs, and keep everything organised by person.

Smart Reminders

Never miss an appointment again. Get push and email reminders at the right time — customisable per event, per family member, per urgency level.

Mobile-First Access

Fully responsive and built for mobile. Access your family's schedule from anywhere — on the school run, at the GP, or during the commute home.

How it works
Up and running in minutes
Step 01

Create your family profile

Sign up and set up your household — add your name, your kids, and any co-parents or other adults you want to include.

Step 02

Invite family members

Send a link to your partner, co-parent, or older kids. Everyone gets their own login and sees only what's relevant to them.

Step 03

Add your events

Start logging school dates, medical appointments, activities, and reminders. Import from Google or Apple Calendar to get started instantly.

Step 04

Stay on top of everything

FCC keeps everyone in sync. Reminders go out automatically, schedules stay updated, and nothing falls through the cracks.
